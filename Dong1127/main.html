Boston has 5957 Airbnb listings and 2856 Airbnb hosts in Sept 2018, according to the data from Airbnb Insider.


Each circle represents 10 Airbnb listings, color represents their price range.

●Not more than $100
●$101 to $200
●$201 to $300
●More than $300

88 percent of Airbnb hosts in the City of Boston rented out 2 units or fewer. This supports the Airbnb company line that the majority of users are average joes renting out their homes. The other 12 percent of hosts, however, listed from 3 to 320 units. They were responsible for 48 percent of all the listings in the city.

However, units listed by those Airbnb investor have a higher average daily rents than those by regular hosts.

They concentrated in the downtown area, including Back Bay, Chinatown, and Fenway, while those from regular hosts distribute evenly.

The average daily rent for investor units is $205. While for those regular Airbnb units, the average daily rent is$159.

Neighborhoods like Back Bay, Dorchester, South End, and Jamaica Plain are Airbnb concentrated.

While in Downtown, Fenway, Chinatown , Airbnb seems to have a higher price.
