Snowy January in Massachusetts

When bombogenesis comes

In January 2018, a strong storm headed straight for Massachusetts, causing extreme weather and heavy snow.

But how did it distribute to different counties in MA? And which county have the heaviest snow in January? That's what I want to know.

Look at the dataset

Here are the data I collected from National Oceanic and Atmospheric Adminstration. I collected all the snowfall and rainfall data in MA counties in January for the latest three years.

What I'm going to do

I would like to make an interactive map showing the snowfall on 3 - 6, January to compare different situations in different MA counties.
When hovering the map, it will show a bar/line chart of the snowfall in different counties of MA in January for the latest three years.
